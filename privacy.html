Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how JoinTheArmy.com collects, uses, and protects personal information submitted through this website.

Effective date: March 19, 2026

1. Who we are

JoinTheArmy.com is an independent English-language online project focused on discipline, structure, identity, and self-leadership. It is not affiliated with any military, government, or recruitment organization.

2. Information we collect

When you use the waitlist or contact features on this website, we may collect the information you choose to provide, including:

  • name

  • interest/preferences, if voluntarily provided

We may use your information to:

  • manage the waitlist

  • inform you about launches, previews, or relevant future offers

By submitting your information through this website, you consent to the processing of that information for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. Where applicable law requires a different or additional legal basis, we will rely on the appropriate legal basis available to us, including consent, legitimate interest, or the performance of pre-contractual steps at your request.

If you join our waitlist or otherwise provide your email address, we may contact you with project updates, launch announcements, selected previews, and relevant information related to JoinTheArmy.com.

You may unsubscribe from non-essential email communications at any time by using the unsubscribe link, where available, or by contacting us at info@jointhearmy.com.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only where reasonably necessary:

By using this website and submitting your information, you understand and accept that such transfers may occur, subject to reasonable safeguards where applicable.

9. Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including to manage the waitlist, maintain communications, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights relating to your personal information, including the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or data portability.

To make a request, contact us at info@jointhearmy.com. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact: info@jointhearmy.com
